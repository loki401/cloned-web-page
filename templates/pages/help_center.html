Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by:

  • Going to "My Orders" in your account
  • Clicking on the order you want to track
  • Viewing real-time tracking information
  • Getting SMS and email updates
What is the return policy?

Our return policy includes:

  • 7-30 days return window depending on product category
  • Free pickup for most returns
  • Refund processed within 7-10 business days
  • Items must be in original condition with tags
How do I cancel my order?

To cancel your order:

  • Go to "My Orders" section
  • Find the order you want to cancel
  • Click "Cancel" if the option is available
  • Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Net Banking from all major banks
  • UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.)
  • Wallets (Flipkart Pay Later, Paytm, etc.)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • EMI options available
How do I redeem a gift card?

To redeem your gift card:

  • Go to "Gift Cards" section in your account
  • Enter your gift card number and security code
  • Click "Add to Account"
  • The amount will be added to your Flipkart wallet
  • Use it during checkout for any purchase
Is it safe to shop on Flipkart?

Yes, shopping on Flipkart is completely safe:

  • SSL encryption for secure transactions
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • Buyer protection policies
  • Verified sellers and authentic products
  • 24/7 customer support
