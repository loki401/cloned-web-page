Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order by going to "My Orders" section in your account or using the order ID sent to your email.

What is the return policy?

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Check the product page for specific return policies.

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it's shipped from "My Orders" section. Once shipped, you can return it after delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, wallets, and cash on delivery.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2-7 business days. Express delivery is available for select products and locations.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and payment information.

How do I change my delivery address?

You can change the delivery address before the order is shipped from "My Orders" section.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Contact our customer care immediately with photos of the damaged item. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
